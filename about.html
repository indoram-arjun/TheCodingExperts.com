Our Journey

How We Grew

From a small classroom to Ballabgarh's #1 coding institute — here's our story.

2019

🚀 The Beginning

The Coding Expert was founded in Sector-3, Ballabgarh with a vision to provide affordable, quality coding education to students of the Faridabad region.

2020

💻 Going Online

Launched fully online classes during COVID-19, ensuring no student missed their education. This marked our shift to a hybrid learning model that students loved.

2021

📚 Expanding Courses

Introduced Python, Digital Marketing, and Advanced Excel courses to our curriculum. Crossed 100 enrolled students and hired our 3rd faculty member.

2023

🏆 #1 in Ballabgarh

Crossed 300+ students and received the highest Google rating in Ballabgarh. Our placement success rate reached 90%+ for job-seeking students.

2025

🎯 500+ Students Strong

Today we serve 500+ students, offer 20+ courses, and have 4 expert faculty members. We continue to grow and evolve to serve the students of Faridabad better every day.
