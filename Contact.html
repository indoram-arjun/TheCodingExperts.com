FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer online classes? +
Yes! We offer both online and offline classes. You can attend live sessions from the comfort of your home or visit our institute. Recorded sessions are also provided for all courses.
Is there a free demo class? +
Yes! We provide 3 free demo classes for all our courses. You can attend before deciding to enroll — no payment required and no commitment.
What certificate will I receive? +
Upon successful completion of a course, you receive a certificate from The Coding Expert. This certificate can be shared on your resume and LinkedIn profile to demonstrate your skills.
Do you provide placement support? +
Yes, we provide 100% placement support! We help students prepare for job interviews, build their portfolios with live projects, and connect them with relevant opportunities in IT.
What are the course fees? +
Our course fees are very affordable and vary by course. Please call us on +91 8595210430 or visit the institute for the latest fee details. We also offer flexible payment options.
Can I join mid-month or start anytime? +
Yes! We have morning, afternoon, and evening batches so you can find a slot that works for you. New batches start regularly and you can join at any convenient time.
