FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling.

Do you offer online classes? +

Yes! We offer both online and offline classes. Online students get access to live sessions, recorded videos, and study materials via WhatsApp and our online portal.

Is there a free demo class? +

Absolutely. We offer 3 free demo classes for every course — no payment, no commitment. Just attend and see if we're the right fit for you.

What certificate do I get after completing a course? +

You receive an institute-level completion certificate with your name, course details, and performance grade. This can be added to your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Do you provide placement support? +

Yes, we offer 100% placement support including mock interviews, resume building, live projects and job referrals to our network of hiring partners.

What are the batch timings? +

We have multiple batches: morning (9 AM), afternoon (1 PM), evening (5 PM & 7 PM). We also arrange flexible timing batches based on student availability.

How much do courses cost? +

Our fees are very affordable and vary by course. Please contact us on WhatsApp or call +91 8595210430 for the current fee structure and any ongoing offers.
